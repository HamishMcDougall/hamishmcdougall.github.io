PROFILE

Experience design leader, product design specialist & product owner with a proven ability to present clear strategic direction, combining the needs and requirements of both internal and external customers, as well as the hands-on experience to execute large scale projects. I have experience in financial services, FMCG both internationally and within Australia.

I have a passion for creating and delivering digital solutions within highly complex and regulated industries. I have delivered the results of my customer research and design to all levels of management, including CEO and board-level members.
