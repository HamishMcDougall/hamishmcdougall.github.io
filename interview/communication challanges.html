Riders - in a digital fromat

I will speak about this as it show communication with customers and

with the ET In the past for a long time it was thought of internally that apps where similar to websites

They were thought of as in pages

I remember having trouble communicating that while TAC was only under 10 pages it was not about the pages

But about the components

Take for example the quoting page.

Its a single page that can be in a number of configurations

This was quite the challenge communicating that we should think and develop in terms of components

To solve this communication challenge I did the following:

1. Talk in components ( such as the Life insurance card, the policy price display component )

2. Reinforce the idea by setting up component style guides within our prototypes and wireframes

This consisted of a large page where all the components required for the page were outlined

with all the possible states and later UI design

Over time by showing not telling we were able to communicate the value of thinking about components

Which allowed us to talk about specific parts of the application and dig down into the required behaviours and design

And allowed me to plan better, and communicate when the wireframes / components would be finished
